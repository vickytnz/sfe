Based on your answers, you can apply for student finance in the UK. This is because:

  • you are a British national
  • you will be living in England at the start of your course
  • you have lived in the UK for 3 years before the start of your course

Supporting information that you will need when you apply

To prove that you are eligible for student finance, when you apply you will need to give:

  • proof of your nationality - either the details from your UK passport or birth certificate
  • the address of where you will live on the first day of your course (or if you move to study, the place you lived before you moved to study)
  • addresses for the 3 years before you started your course
  • National Insurance number - this is used to check your details with the Department for Work and Pensions.

You may also need to give extra information and supporting evidence to get some loans and grants, for example:

  • previous study details
  • course details
  • household income
  • details about children you wish to get support for while studying
