Getting student finance in England depends on:

  • your nationality or immigration status
  • your permanent home before your course ('ordinary residence')
  • where you have lived in the 3 years (or sometimes 7 years) before your course

Use this tool to check:

  • if you can apply for student finance
  • what information and supporting documents you may need when you apply to prove that you can get student finance

Even if you can apply for student finance, you may only be able to get some grants or loans. You can get an estimate of what you can get by using the student finance calculator.
