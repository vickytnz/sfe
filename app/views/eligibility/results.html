Based on your answers, you can apply for student finance from Student Finance England. This is because:

  • you are a British national
  • your permanent home at the start of your course is England
  • in the 3 years before the start of your course you have only lived in the UK, Channel Islands, or Isle of Man

Supporting information that you will need when you apply

To prove that you can apply for student finance, when you apply you will need to give:

  • proof of your nationality - either the details from your UK passport or a copy of your birth certificate
  • the address of your permanent home at the start of your course
  • addresses of where you lived for the 3 years before you started your course

You may also need to give extra information and supporting evidence to get some loans and grants, for example:

  • your National Insurance number
  • previous study details
  • course details
  • household income
  • details about children you wish to get support for while studying

You will find out what information you need to give when you apply.
